Information

  • Put input into the box(K,C,Q)
  • Use caps lock for the input
  • Remember, fill 4 character per area
  • You can choose 10 of all character
  • Each character's quantity is written beside each points
  • Winner is the player who win more area
  • You can add unrelated letter to input to confuse your opponent with max 4 chars
  • The letter must not be the symbol of classes to make the game fair
  • Good luck have fun

    • Scores

  • (K)ing= 5 (1)
  • (Q)ueen= 5 (1)
  • (C)avalry= 20 times enemy pawns (1)
  • (A)ssassin=5;(autowin when 1 vs 1) (1)
  • (S)niper=35(1)
  • (P)awns=@1;(4 Pawns in an area will raise the points into 30 (4)
  • (R)ebel=1;(autowin vs royalty if teamed up with 2 pawns or more) (1)
  • (B)omber= Win the area. A trump card that only can be countered with draw (1)

    • Skills

  • King in presence with Queen make total points of 30
  • 4 Pawn gathers in 1 area make total point of 40
  • Assassin will perform instant kill when 1 vs 1 in an area
  • Bomber nukes whole area
  • Rebel wins the area if there is an enemy royalty such as King and Queen and teamed up with 2 pawn.

    • Rules

  • Game system by round plays.
  • P1 moves first then P2 for first area.
  • P2 moves first then P1 for second area, and goes on.
  • After each player submit their moves, reveal the area to declare the winner.
  • You can predict what chars havent been used and outplay the enemy.
